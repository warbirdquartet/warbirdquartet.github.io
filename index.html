Miserytown - The Story of the Warbird Quartet

Over the summer of 2009, the Warbird Quartet starred in a rockumentary, entitled Miserytown - The Story of the Warbird Quartet. Samantha Povilonis directed the film, which was created for the first annual Elgin Short Film Festival in 2009. The video below is Miserytown in its entirety. An HD version will be uploaded in the next few days.

Thanks to everyone who helped us with the project. We know it wouldn't be nearly as good if it weren't for the great performances from all of our actors.

Also, thanks to Suzy McQuen for funding the film festival entry.
